# Pet Sphere 🐾🐶

An all-in-one web application for pet lovers providing a centralized 
digital platform to purchase animals, book veterinary appointments, 
and order medicines — all in one place.

## 🌐 Live Demo
🔗 https://petsphere.infinityfree.me


## 📋 About the Project
Pet Sphere is a comprehensive pet care platform designed to make pet 
ownership digital and hassle-free. Users can purchase animals with 
specific preferences, consult qualified veterinary doctors, track pet 
health records, and order prescribed medicines — all from within the app. 
The platform features live doctor availability, online consultations, 
digital prescriptions, an emergency SOS system, and AI-powered product 
recommendations.


## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 User Panel
- Secure registration and login
- Profile management
- Animal wishlist and order history
- Browse pets with advanced filters (species, breed, color, age, price)
- Custom pet request with specific preferences
- Cart and checkout system
- AI product recommendations based on pet breed, age, or health
- Subscription model for pet food and medications
- Verified breeder and store reviews and ratings

### 🏥 Veterinary Doctors Module
- Doctor profiles with specialization and experience
- Live availability status (Available / Busy / Offline)
- Appointment booking system
- Online consultations via chat and video
- Display consultation fees
- Online payment via JazzCash / Easypaisa
- Digital medical prescriptions

### 🐾 Pet Health Records
- Pet Passport — vaccination history, allergies, and medical records
- Automated vaccination and medicine reminders

### 🆘 Emergency Support System
- SOS button to alert nearby vets, shelters, or pet ambulance services
- Built-in first-aid guide for first-time pet owners

### ⚙️ Admin Panel
- Manage users, animals, doctors, and medicines
- Set appointments and track orders
- Reports and analytics (sales, bookings, active users)
- Doctor verification and status management
